Pro Tips

Resume Tips from the Pros

Actionable advice from recruiters and hiring managers to help your resume stand out.

1

Tailor Your Resume for Every Job

Mirror the exact keywords and skills from the job description. ATS systems scan for keyword matches, so alignment is critical.

2

Lead With Impact, Not Duties

Replace vague duty descriptions with quantified achievements: Action Verb + What You Did + Measurable Result.

3

Optimize for ATS

Use standard section headings, avoid tables and graphics, and save as PDF. Most companies use ATS to filter resumes before a human sees them.

4

Use Strong Action Verbs

Start every bullet with power verbs like Spearheaded, Orchestrated, Delivered, Accelerated. Avoid passive language.
